Current Project

Book The Whole F***ing World

Client: Timebomb Productions
Salt Lake City, UT

Overview

The process of booking live concerts and events, especially independent ones, has not changed in the past few decades. It is always word of mouth. It is always who you know.

The purpose of this web application is to help would-be concert promoters and bands more easily find venues, promoters and other bands for these events.

A little back story: There was once a printed magazine ("zine") called BYOFL, or Book Your Own F***ing Life that helped people looking to get involved in live music. That only lasted a few years and nothing ever took its place.

Role: UX/UI Design, UI Developer, Product Owner

Status: Front-End Development

"After all this time, indie shows are still being booked this way?!"

I took some time away from music while I raised my son and when I started playing in a band and performing again, I was astounded that nothing has changed. We are surrounded by all manner of technology and information overload these days and yet, there are still musicians and promoters who have no idea how to get started.

Client: Division 6
Raleigh, NC

Overview

What if you could leverage an influencer's popularity in the form of a commodity in a market based on social personalities? That's what Influence.inc is trying to determine.
